# Blood Product Transport Protocol — DroneMedic Standard Operating Procedure

## Temperature Requirements
- Whole blood and red blood cells: 2-6°C
- Platelets: 20-24°C with agitation
- Fresh frozen plasma: -18°C or below (thaw before use)
- Cryoprecipitate: -18°C or below

## Chain of Custody Requirements
1. Blood bank verification (product ID, patient match)
2. Temperature logger activation
3. Sealed transport container
4. Drone payload bay inspection
5. Continuous temperature monitoring during flight
6. Landing zone verification
7. Handoff to authorized medical personnel
8. Recipient verification (name, role, ID)
9. Temperature check on receipt (must be within range)

## Maximum Transport Time
- Red blood cells: 30 minutes without active cooling
- Platelets: 4 hours at room temperature
- Plasma (thawed): 24 hours at 1-6°C

## Emergency Procedures
- If temperature exceeds 10°C: ABORT delivery, return to base
- If container integrity compromised: Log incident, notify blood bank
- If recipient unavailable: Hold at location max 15 minutes, then return
